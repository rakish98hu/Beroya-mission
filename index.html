Beroya Mission High School (BMHS) is an educational Institution that provides care and education for both Boys and Girls. We provide quality mental, physical and spiritual education to the youth, bring the youth together through team-work in academics , Sports and Culture regardless of gender, status, race, religion and political ideology.
